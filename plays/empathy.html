Understand users with empathy mapping

Running the play

Empathy mapping helps you derive insights from your user research and foster a greater awareness of, and empathy for, your users. You can use this play to catalog and group your users' needs.

Empathy mapping relies heavily on a set of user personas. If you don't have access to pre-defined personas, STOP RIGHT HERE. Without a codified picture of what this person's motivations and responsibilities are, everyone in the group will bring their own opinions and biases into the discussion

Access the Empathy Mapping play in the “The Atlassian Team Playbook”, by Atlassian (2019).
