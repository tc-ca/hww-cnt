TC Digital Play on Managing Virtual Teams

Support middle-managers in managing virtual teams by providing them with tips and tricks to create culture change.

Use this play to:

  • Support middle-managers in managing virtual teams by providing them with tips and tricks to manage this new working environment(i.e dealing with staff crisis, team spirit, productivity, etc.)

Running the play:

  • Managing virtual teams can be challenging and requires a more organized approach (i.e. Regularly define work expectations and deliverables as well as communicating them, scheduling regular meetings, etc.).
  • Managing is a skill set that should be matured and supported on an ongoing basis.
